    Literary Favorites

    • How To Talk So Kids Will Listen & Listen So Kids Will Talk

      • A surprisingly insightful book on communication! My mom recommended it to me and I was skeptical at first but it does a great job at describing in simple terms how to make people feel seen and heard

    • Wolfpack

      • An inspiring book on empowerment. You'll read it in an hour but it will provide motivation for months. The messages on cultivating and supporting your team are short and sweet.

    • My Stupid Intentions

      • This book was pretty strange! It follows a beech marten (a type of weasel) on his search for purpose. The author grapples with both the positives and negatives of surviving versus thriving.

    • The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

      • Super fun read! Evelyn is a wonderful character and who doesn't enjoy the drama of Hollywood. They also tie in a lot of historical events and give some really interesting perspective into that time.

    • Maybe You Should Talk To Someone

      • I didn't expect to be laughing so much at a book about therapy but here we are.
